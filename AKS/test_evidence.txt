COMPLIANCE AUDIT EVIDENCE - ACCESS CONTROL POLICY
Reference: ACC-2026-V1.0
Entity: Global Finance Corp

This document outlines the corporate Access Control and Identity Management policies as required by regulatory compliance frameworks.

1. Governance Framework
The Executive Board oversees all security governance. Security policies are reviewed annually to align with industry standard frameworks.

2. Access Control & Identity Management
- All administrator accounts must utilize Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA/2FA) for logging in.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and contain uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
- Access privileges are granted under the principle of least privilege and reviewed quarterly.

3. Security Training & Awareness
All employees must undergo mandatory cybersecurity training upon onboarding and annually thereafter.

4. Cryptography and Key Management
Sensitive customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. Cryptographic keys are rotated every 90 days.
